Trip to Virginia and Maryland

Welcome to Nandini's Travel Blog

This website is to portray my travel experience to Virgina and Maryland. Virginia is home to some of the top tourist attractions and landmarks in the United States, offering plenty of things to see and do for all interests. Among these Luray Caverns and Virginia Safari Park are the places one must visit in East Coast of America. The world-famous Baltimore's National Aquarium which sees over 1.6 visitors per year. I have visited this place three times so far and always feel like visiting this place as many time as I can. I hope you will like it too!

Luray Caverns, VA

Luray Caverns are the largest caverns in the eastern USA.It was a indeed a mind-boggling experience visiting to this caverns. These caverns have some of the most spectacular creations which were formed just one drip at time, such as Giant's Hall, vast expansive chambers decorated by predominantly golden columns, 10-stories tall."

Virginia Safari Park, VA

Virginia Safari Park is the Virginia's only Drive-Thru Safari.This park is the only place in the United States where we can see the rare king Cheetah.It is truly fun filling trip for the entire family.In the entire zoo visit my most exciting experience was the animal feeding.

National Aquarium, Maryland

National Aquarium located in Baltimore, Maryland is ranked as one of the nation's top three aquariums, the aquarium offers awe-inspiring exhibits that are sure to bring astonishing experience to visitors.Located in Baltimore’s Inner Harbor, we’ll find thousands of species in award-winning habitats. From colorful aquatic characters like sharks, dolphins, exotic fish to diverse collections of plants and animals like crocodiles, birds and reptiles, the National Aquarium delivers a memorable experience for people of any age.The main attarction here was the Dolphin show, which was the mesmerizing experience for me.
